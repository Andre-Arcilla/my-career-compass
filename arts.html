Theater Arts/
Performing Arts
The Theater Arts and Design track is a specialized educational program that teaches students about dramatic arts, including acting, stagecraft, playwriting, and history, preparing them for careers in acting, directing, or stage management.
Specialized Subjects:
  • Performing Arts Production
  • Leadership and Management in Different Arts Fields
  • Apprenticeship and Exploration in the Performing Arts
  • Creative Industries I: Arts and Design Appreciation and Production
  • Integrating Elements and Principles of the Different Arts and Applications
