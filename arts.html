Theater Arts/
Performing Arts
The Theater Arts and Design track is a specialized educational program that teaches students about dramatic arts, including acting, stagecraft, playwriting, and history, preparing them for careers in acting, directing, or stage management.
Specialized Subjects:
  • Performing Arts Production
  • Leadership and Management in Different Arts Fields
  • Apprenticeship and Exploration in the Performing Arts
  • Creative Industries I: Arts and Design Appreciation and Production
  • Integrating Elements and Principles of the Different Arts and Applications
    • Possible Courses:
  • AB in Communication Studies major in Theater Arts
  • BS in Performing Arts
  • BS in Speech and Theater Arts
    • Possible Universities:
  • Meridian International College
  • Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela
  • Philippine Normal University
    • Career Opportunities:(5)
  • Drama Coach
  • Producer
  • Director and Stage Manager

    • Music
    Students' musical abilities are enhanced through theory, performance, and technology in the Arts & Design track with a music major. Vocal/instrumental instruction, theory classes, ensemble concerts, and composition projects are among the activities. The program's goal is to deepen students' musical understanding and practical skills, preparing them for careers in music performances and compositions.
    Specialized Subjects:
  • Music Composition
  • Instrumental Performance
  • Music Production and Technology
  • Vocal Performance
  • Music: General and Private Education

  • Bachelor of Music in Piano Accompanying and Chamber Music
